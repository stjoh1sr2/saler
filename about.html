About Saler

Our project, Garage Saler, is a website for garage sale hosts and potential customers. Users will be able to go on the website to view different garage sale listings. These listings will have information such as the address, general categories of items (clothing, movies, etc.), as well as pictures of the sale. This project will also allow for users to host ads of their own garage sales by adding information about their sale to be viewed by others on the website. When viewing available listings, users can add them to a cart feature, from which the website can generate the most efficient route for the user to travel to each of the sales they chose. Through all these features, we aim to modernize the experience of garage sale hunting.
